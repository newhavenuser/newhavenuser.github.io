[Curriculum Vitae]


I am an assistant professor in Centre for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (CAIR), Chinese Academy of Sciences. Before joining CAIR, I received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering, City University of Hong Kong (CityU) in 2022, and worked as postdoc fellow, advised by Prof. Yixuan Yuan. Prior to that, I received the M.Phil. degree in Electronic Engineering and Information Science, MOE-Microsoft Key Laboratory of Multimedia Computing and Communication, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2019, and the B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering, Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU) in 2016, with admission to XJTU-CASIA Automation Elite Class.

My research interests include AI for Healthcare, Medical Image Analysis, and Multimodal Learning. Recently I focus on multi-modal LLMs for diagnosis and surgery. In recognition of the research achievements in AI-assisted diagnosis and surgery, I received the Hong Kong Young Scientist Award 2023, as the only awardee in the engineering field of Hong Kong.

I am looking for research collaboration, please drop me an email if you are interested.
